# CapCut Account Sharing System - User Guide

## Welcome to CapCut Account Sharing

This platform allows multiple users to share a single CapCut Pro account through automated session management. Book your 1.5-hour editing session and receive login credentials when your time slot begins.

---

## How It Works

### 1. Session Booking Process
1. **Enter Your Details** - Provide your name and email address
2. **Select Time Slot** - Choose from available 30-minute intervals (6 AM - 10:30 PM)
3. **Confirm Booking** - Receive booking confirmation with session details
4. **Get Credentials** - Receive CapCut login details via email when your session starts

### 2. Session Duration
- **Session Length**: 1.5 hours per booking
- **Advance Booking**: Book up to 24 hours in advance
- **Time Zones**: All times displayed in your local timezone

### 3. Account Access
- **New Password**: Generated automatically for each session
- **Email Delivery**: Credentials sent to your email when session starts
- **Account Sharing**: Same CapCut Pro account with full features

---

## Step-by-Step Booking Guide

### Step 1: Access the Platform
Visit the booking platform at: `http://localhost:3000` (or your deployed URL)

### Step 2: Enter Your Information
Fill out the booking form:
- **Full Name**: Your display name for the booking
- **Email Address**: Where you'll receive login credentials

**Important**: Use a valid email address as this is where you'll receive your CapCut login details.

### Step 3: Choose Your Time Slot

#### Available Time Periods
- **Morning**: 6:00 AM - 12:00 PM
- **Afternoon**: 12:00 PM - 6:00 PM  
- **Evening**: 6:00 PM - 10:30 PM

#### Slot Status Indicators
- **✓ Green**: Available for booking
- **✗ Red**: Already booked by another user
- **Past**: Time slot has already passed

#### Time Slot Rules
- Sessions start every 30 minutes
- Each session lasts exactly 1.5 hours
- Last session starts at 10:30 PM and ends at 12:00 AM

### Step 4: Confirm Your Booking
Review your booking details:
- **Time Slot**: Start and end times
- **Contact Details**: Your name and email
- **Session ID**: Unique identifier for your booking

Click "Confirm & Book" to complete your reservation.

### Step 5: Booking Confirmation
You'll see a confirmation screen with:
- **Session Details**: Your reserved time slot
- **Session ID**: Keep this for your records
- **Next Steps**: What to expect before your session

## Receiving Your Credentials

### Email Notification
**When**: You'll receive an email 2-3 minutes before your session starts
**Content**: 
- CapCut login email address
- Temporary password (valid only for your session)
- Login instructions
- Session end time reminder

### Email Example
```
Subject: Your CapCut Session is Starting - Session #sess_123

Hello [Your Name],

Your CapCut Pro session is starting now!

Login Details:
Email: daevid621@gmail.com
Password: SecurePass123!

Your session runs from 2:00 PM to 3:30 PM today.

Please log out when your session ends to ensure the next user can access the account.

Need help? Contact support at support@capcutsharing.com

Best regards,
CapCut Sharing Platform
```

### No Email Received?
If you don't receive your credentials email:
1. **Check Spam/Junk Folder**: Automated emails sometimes end up here
2. **Verify Email Address**: Ensure you entered the correct email during booking
3. **Contact Support**: Use the session ID to get assistance

## Booking Rules and Policies

### Booking Limitations
- **One Session Per User**: Limit of 1 active booking per email address
- **No Same-Day Changes**: Cannot modify or cancel bookings on the same day
- **24-Hour Advance**: Book up to 24 hours in advance
- **Fair Usage**: Maximum 3 sessions per user per week

### Session Policies
- **Punctuality**: Sessions start promptly at the scheduled time
- **No Extensions**: Sessions cannot be extended beyond the 1.5-hour limit
- **No-Show Policy**: Unused sessions cannot be rescheduled
- **Account Security**: Do not share login credentials with others

### Prohibited Activities
- **No Account Sharing**: Don't give credentials to other people
- **No Password Changes**: Don't attempt to change the account password
- **No Personal Information**: Don't add personal payment methods or details
- **Commercial Use**: Limited to personal/educational projects only

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### "No Available Slots"
- **Cause**: All time slots for today are already booked
- **Solution**: Try booking for tomorrow or check back later for cancellations

#### "Booking Failed" Error
- **Cause**: The selected time slot was booked by someone else
- **Solution**: Refresh the page and select a different available slot

#### "Invalid Email" Error  
- **Cause**: Email address format is incorrect
- **Solution**: Use a valid email format (e.g., user@example.com)

#### Login Credentials Don't Work
- **Possible Causes**:
  - Session hasn't started yet
  - Incorrect password entry
  - Session has already ended
- **Solutions**:
  - Wait until your session start time
  - Copy and paste the password from the email
  - Check if your session time has passed

#### "Session Not Found"
- **Cause**: Invalid session ID or booking doesn't exist
- **Solution**: Double-check your booking confirmation email

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: How much does this cost?**
A: The booking platform is free to use. You're sharing the cost of a CapCut Pro subscription.

**Q: Can I book multiple sessions?**
A: Yes, but only one session at a time. You can book your next session after completing your current one.

**Q: What if I finish my project early?**
A: You can log out early, but the session time cannot be transferred to another user or saved for later.

**Q: Can I save my projects permanently?**
A: Projects are saved to the shared account temporarily. Export your work before your session ends.

### Technical Questions

**Q: What internet speed do I need?**
A: CapCut Pro works best with a stable internet connection of at least 10 Mbps for video editing.

**Q: Can I use CapCut on mobile during my session?**
A: The login credentials work for web, desktop, and mobile versions of CapCut.

**Q: What happens if the website is down?**
A: Check our status page or contact support. Session time may be extended if there's a system outage.

**Q: Can I extend my session if I'm not finished?**
A: No, sessions are strictly 1.5 hours. Plan your editing work accordingly.

### Account Questions

**Q: What if I accidentally change account settings?**
A: Don't modify account settings. If you do accidentally change something, contact support immediately.

**Q: Can I see other users' projects?**
A: Projects are generally separated, but avoid opening or modifying projects you didn't create.

**Q: What if I get locked out during my session?**
A: Contact support immediately with your session ID for assistance.
